Our Mission

Within the first two weeks of April 2020, researchers documented over 1,135 cases of targeted harassment abuse against Asian Americans, a shocking reversal of previous trends. In a time of ever-increasing division, there is a desperate need to preserve, recognize, and highlight the untold stories of Asian American heroes, both past and present. Amongst these heroes are the oft-overlooked “Chinese Yankees,” young Asian Americans who participated in the most pivotal battles of the American Civil War. Users are matched with the profile of a real life “Chinese Yankee,” and immersed in their Civil War story through a memorable, state-of-the-art AI-powered interactive experience. We bring powerful stories to life and update them to the digital age.
